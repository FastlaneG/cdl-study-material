CDL Study Guide

Section 2.1

1. What is the most important reason for doing a vehicle inspection?

A. Safety

2. What things should you check during a trip?

A. Tires, wheels and rims. Brakes, lights and reflectors.

3. Name some key steering system parts

A. Steering wheel, steering shaft, gear box.

4. Name some suspension system defects

A. Cracked or broken spring hangers, leaking shock absorbers, broken leaves.

5. What three kinds of emergency equipment must you have?

A. Fire extinguishers, spare electrical fuses, 3 reflective warning triangles.

6. What is the minimum tread depth for front tires? For other tires?

A. At least 4/32 inch tread depth on front tires. At least 2/32 inch on other tires.
